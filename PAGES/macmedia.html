About this repository

For this example to test the project, all three pages must be open so that the effect and collaboration can be seen. On the checkout page, the employee/customer can place their order. The individual and total prices are displayed, plus you can also remove products. After clicking the 'checkout' button, the order goes to the employee who sees the order and can start preparing it for the customer. This specific page refreshes every 3 seconds to ensure it is actually updated with the database. After completing the order, the employee can click the 'delete' button, and this order is moved from 'busy' to 'ready' on the viewing page so that the waiting customer can see that their order is ready.
